The Five Stages of Life

April 25, 2022

I have been reflecting on my life journey recently and wanted to write about my experience. I see human life developing through five distinct stages, each stage separated by a change in incentives. Stage 1 is biological driven mimicry, stage 2 is parental driven mimicry, stage 3 is peer driven mimicry, stage 4 is self-discovery and stage 5 is commitment. The first two stage transitions are mostly biologically driven with the last two stage transitions being mostly intellectually driven. The transitions are usually gradual over time and people can be in multiple stages at once. Future generations should focus on transitioning to stage 4 prior to entering high school and being comfortably in stage 5 before entering a lifelong relationship. These will be difficult but worthwhile goals.

Zac's Camper Van Build

April 18, 2022

After finishing a house remodel around Q2 2021, I finally got around to building out my 2020 Ford Transit. The internet covered most of the heavy lifting as it had been accumulating modern camper van builds since roughly 2006. FarOutRide, bless their souls, provides an amazing resource at the best possible price, free. Check them out for build details and please use their affiliate links when possible! Always enjoying a challenge, I required that my build have all the conveniences of a house while also being off-grid capable for over a week. The van is roughly 90% complete.
